In 2021 a new era of space colonization - space tourism - has come. Private companies has obtained an access to Space.

A competition between them has sharpened, and the cost of tickets to the Space decreased in hundred times.

On July 11th 2021, Virgin Galactic company has carried out the first "tourist" suborbital flight with its founder - British multimillionaire, Richard Branson, on board. A spaceship Unity 22has departed from space port America in the state of New Mexico by means of mother aircraft VMS Eve. Later it was disconnected from an mother aicraft. The management of Virgin Galactic hopes that mass suborbital space flights will start before 2022.
